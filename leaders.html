Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela was the first Black president of South Africa and a global symbol of peace and freedom. He fought against apartheid and spent 27 years in prison. After his release, he led South Africa into democracy and unity.

Queen Elizabeth II

Queen Elizabeth II ruled the UK for over 70 years, representing stability, tradition, and leadership during major global changes.

Napoleon Bonaparte

Napoleon Bonaparte was a French military leader and Emperor, known for his victories, leadership, and major influence on European history.
